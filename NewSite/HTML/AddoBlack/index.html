Travel Blog

Monaco & Côte d’Azur

For a few days my friend MarcO and I stayed in Monaco and checked “gamble in Monte Carlo” off the bucket list. Monaco proper is quite expensive, but also beautiful and every bit worth visiting.

Venice, Italy

Venice is the most beautiful city I have even been, period. There are no cars. There is less noise. The food is incredible. If I spoke a lick of Italian then I would be living there as we speak.

Sao Miguel, Azores

I got to Sao Miguel by googling “cheap flights from Boston to Europe”. In steps SETA airlines for a very cheap and short flight. While it isn’t mainland Europe, it is its own unique adventure island.
