Homegrown Host

Amy at Homegrown Host wanted to rebrand an event planning company targeted at high end southern events hosted by women. Amy needed something simple, elegant, and hand made. One of her requirements what that it include a pineapple, the symbol of hospitality in the south. During our initial meeting Amy made it clear that she wanted her new identity to have that personal look and feel while still conveying professionalism and class.

For her new logo and brand elements, a few different options were presented. Bright colors and the slightly offset fill ended up being exactly what she was looking for to bring her new brand exactly where she wanted it. After a few iterations back and forth we landed on what you see here as a final decisions. From there I produced a logo, sample website (she was not ready to launch a full one yet), and some marketing material like business cards and one sheets that explain her services.

Amy was extremely happy with the way her brand identity turned out. The traditional southern elegance mixed with the handmade detail oriented event planner made for the perfect blend for Homegrown Host.
