Meet The Team

Brian Desmarais

Owner

Brian grew up in a small rural town in New Hampshire and out of High School joined the Army in 2002. During the 4 years of service the Real Estate seed was planted. After the Military he moved to West Chester PA to pursue a Bachelors Degree in Economics. During his time at West Chester University he also met his wife Katie who grew up in Saucon Valley. The pair opted to leave West Chester to move to the Lehigh Valley to start a family and in 2020 introduced their first daughter Madison to the world.

Jeremy Nolt

Owner

He fell in love with “The Valley” and immersed himself in the local culture of the area.
