Mystic Perks

Assembly Row wanted to change from handing out a plastic card that was used for discounts for the people living and working there to a more modern and measurable solution. They wanted to take the card out of the wallet and put it on the phone. They also needed a more intelligent way to track where the card is being used and how often.

We landed on an adaptive web app using google's firebase to make the solution. For our end users we would have them enter a few things like their email and verification that they lived or worked at the site, and for the admins they would have a place to log in and view usage stats as well as the user info in case they opted in to other marketing material being sent to them.

After designing the UI elements, user experience flows, and technology we would use at point of sale, we built out the administrative console where the Assembly Row employees could manage users, deals, and view usage statistics. This enables them to use data to support their sales pitches to local businesses as well as add another "perk" to someone moving into the area's apartment complex.

Overall, Assembly Row was very happy with the project and plans to continue to work with me for other future projects they have. Their web app remains in use today with hundreds of business deals, thousands of discounts given, and over five thousand users.
