Grids & Glyphs

The design blog branch of Let's Move Mountains goes by the name Grids & Glyphs. I created this using WordPress and a customized theme. All of the work posted is presented with the design blurb from the firm or person who created it (if there was one) as well as a link to their portfolio.

I was excited to start up Grids & Glyphs after years of following along with other great blogs like BP&O, FormFiftyFive, and Brand New. I love drawing inspiration from others' work, and I am happy to help spread some great design work around the web.

If you would like to submit a link to have something featured please email hello (at) letsmovemountains (dot) com. Thank you and enjoy. Grids & Glyphs
